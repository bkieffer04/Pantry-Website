Pantry: Events and Groceries
Pantry is your go-to app for creating and managing social events such as parties, celebrations, and roommate gatherings. Each event allows users to collaboratively plan by adding items they are bringing, requesting, or noting allergies to ensure everyone stays informed. Our goal is to foster transparency and communication between hosts and guests, making event planning seamless and enjoyable.
